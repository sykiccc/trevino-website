Twelve Pillars by Jim Rohn
Chapter 4

In this chapter Micheal and Charlie are catching up with what has been going on in each other lives. Charlie spoke on the improvements of his relationship with Amy while Micheal spoke about his career. While talking about his career, Micheal revealed to Charlie that he does not feel like he is achieveing his goals. This was then followed by various questions from Charlie to better understand where Micheal's thought process was. After answering these questions Charlie began to relay advice he had heard about goal-setting and how it can improve or change someone's life. Charlie spoke on how writing down your goals is very important and even powerful. This chapter helped in teaching me how essential knowing and writing down your goals is and even how to properly organize them into different sections such as long term and micro.

Chapter 5

Charlie went on to speak about how it is important to remain disciplined. Having a goal is great but working toward it or them daily will determine if you achieve them or not. Charlie quoted Mr. Davis, "We must all suffer from one of two pains: The pain of discipline or the pain of regret. The difference is discipline weighs ounces while regret weighs tons. He then went on to talk about how we must control our days and not let our days control us. As someone that recently has had a lot of things outside my personal goals take over a lot of my time to the point where I feel as if I no longer have the time to do those things that I have set out to do, these chapter has impacted me in a way that I know realize that I have been letting the days control me and not the other way around. The words of both Charlie and Mr. Davis have encouraged me into taking control over my days and therefore my life. If I allow the day to control me or outside factors, I will never achieve the things I want to or have a chance to live the life I want to live.

Chapter 6

Micheal stopped by Charlie's house to spend some time with him and to also learn more about Mr. Davis' Pillar of Success. The topic Charlie chose to speak about is Surround Yourself with the Best. In discussing this topic Charlie talked about how the people we associate ourselves with, especially the ones we associate with the most have an impact of our attitude and how our mindset will be. Additionally, he asked Micheal to evaluate his friends to see if he should disassociate them, limit association or increase association with them. Furthermore, Charlie said that in order to join these groups of successful, committed persons, Micheal himself, will first have to become a successful committed persons. This chapter has enlightened me on how important the people around you are. Although subtle, their association can impact my very life for the negative or positive. In addition to this it was stated that in order to associate myself with successful people I then must be successful. To achieve such success I must be disciplined to my goals, both big and small. WIth associating myself with persons that will push me towards my goal instead of hold me back will impact me tremendously for the better.

Chapter 7

Charlie spoke to Micheal about how education or learning does not stop at the college or university level and that it is the ones that learn after that stage in life that become successful. He further goes into a way that Micheal can associate and learn from the successful persons he strives to both be around and become, which is reading and specifically, reading their books. Learning their very ways and ideas to better himself so that he may achieve his goals. As a current student of a university this chapter served as a kind of wake up call. Not that I ever planned on stopping my education or learning at the university level, however it has reinforced the idea that I must continue learning. You can never have too much wisdom and the acquired knowledge from continuing my education can be the very difference whether I become successful and achieve my dreams or not.
